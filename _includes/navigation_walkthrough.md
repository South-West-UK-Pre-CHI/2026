Directional signs will be posted inside and outside of the building for each room, toilet and lift. 
The Conference is held in Bill Brown Design Suite (room F.101 a/b/c), located on the Basement of Queen’s Building. 
A map of the building is on MazeMap.

The easiest way to enter the conference venue is through the door on Woodland Road, where you can also find the ramp access. 

There will be automatic doors at the entrance which need UCard access. Volunteers will be there to open the door for you.

After you enter the building, the main conference rooms are on your right.

A full navigation walkthrough, with images, can be found in the Access and Inclusion Plan.
